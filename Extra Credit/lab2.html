About Me

As an avid gamer and CS:GO enthusiast, I've been fascinated by the virtual economies that develop around game items. I created this website to share my knowledge about the CS:GO skins marketplace, analyzing trends, and helping others understand the factors that influence skin values. With several years of trading experience, I've developed insights into what makes certain skins retain their value while others depreciate. Through this site, I hope to provide valuable information for both new traders and experienced collectors in the CS:GO community.
