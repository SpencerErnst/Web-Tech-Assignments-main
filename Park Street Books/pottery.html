Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have artistic skills to paint pottery?

Not at all! Our studio is designed for all skill levels. We provide stencils, idea books, and our friendly staff is always available to help with techniques and suggestions.

How long does it take for my pottery to be ready after painting?

Once you've finished painting your pottery, we'll glaze and fire it in our kiln. This process usually takes about 7-10 days. We'll contact you when your masterpiece is ready for pickup!

Is the pottery food-safe after firing?

Yes! All our pottery and glazes are food-safe and dishwasher-safe after firing.

Do I need to make a reservation for in-studio painting?

Walk-ins are welcome for individuals and small groups (up to 4 people). For larger groups or private events, we recommend making a reservation at least one week in advance.

What's included in the to-go pottery kits?

Our to-go kits include selected pottery pieces, paints, brushes, instructions, and everything else you need to create your masterpiece at home. Once you've finished painting, simply return your pieces to us for glazing and firing.

Ready to Get Creative?

Whether you're planning a family outing, a date night, or a solo creative session, The Pottery Place is the perfect destination. No reservations needed for small groups - just stop by during our open hours!
